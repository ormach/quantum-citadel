Free browser game
where you collect cards
to learn physics.

How it works

Each day you get coins to buy cards. Each card represents a physics concept or phenomenon. You use cards to complete quests or keep them to get various bonuses.

  • Learn as you play.
  • Combine cards to get better rewards.
  • Manage your economy to progress faster.

Anki & spaced repetition

If you are familiar with Anki cards, you may also know that spaced repetition is one of the better ways to retain learned material. Quantum Citadel uses a similar method, to prevent you from forgetting what you have learned while playing the game.

Cards & themes

The game has 5 sets of 10 cards that cover introductory physics areas. So far the game covers:

  • Objects: Teaches you about object properties.
  • Gravity: Newton's laws and gravitation.
  • Planetary motion: Kepler's laws.
  • Energy: Teaches you about various energy types.
  • Electricity: Learn about voltage, electrical current and more.
